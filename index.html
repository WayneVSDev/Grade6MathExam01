Grade 6 Mathematics Year-End Mock Exam

Section A: Number and Operations (25 marks)

1. Calculate: 3.45 + 2.67 - 1.89 (3 marks)

2. Simplify the fraction: 36/48 (2 marks)

3. Convert 3/5 to a percentage. (2 marks)

4. Find 15% of 80. (3 marks)

5. If the ratio of boys to girls in a class is 3:5, and there are 24 girls, how many boys are there? (5 marks)

6. Solve: 7x - 3 = 25 (5 marks)

7. Order the following from smallest to largest: 0.75, 3/4, 70%, 0.8 (5 marks)

Section B: Geometry and Measurement (30 marks)

8. Calculate the perimeter of a rectangle with length 8 cm and width 5 cm. (3 marks)

9. Calculate the area of a rectangle with length 8 cm and width 5 cm. (3 marks)

10. Find the volume of a cuboid with length 4 cm, width 3 cm, and height 5 cm. (4 marks)

11. A circular pizza has a diameter of 30 cm. Calculate its circumference. (Use π = 3.14) (5 marks)

12. In a triangle ABC, angle A = 45°, angle B = 60°. What is the measure of angle C? (3 marks)

13. Convert 2.5 kg to grams. (2 marks)

14. A car travels 240 km in 3 hours. What is its average speed in km/h? (5 marks)

Section C: Data and Statistics (20 marks)

15. The following are test scores of 7 students: 85, 92, 78, 95, 88, 90, 83

a) Calculate the mean score. (3 marks)

b) What is the range of the scores? (2 marks)

16. In a survey of 50 students' favorite colors: Red: 15, Blue: 20, Green: 10, Yellow: 5

What percentage of students chose Blue as their favorite color? (10 marks)

17. If you roll a fair six-sided die, what is the probability of rolling an even number? Express as a fraction. (5 marks)

Section D: Problem Solving (25 marks)

18. A recipe for 4 people requires 200g of flour. How much flour would be needed to make the same recipe for 6 people? (8 marks)

19. A rectangular garden is 15m long and 10m wide. A path 1m wide is built around the inside edge of the garden. Calculate the area of the path. (8 marks)

20. John bought a shirt that was on sale for 20% off. If he paid $32 for the shirt, what was its original price? (9 marks)
